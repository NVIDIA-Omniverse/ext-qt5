Usage:
      $ ./simple.pl [options]

    Frobnitz the quux via usage of blargle.

    Crungy factor may be taken into consideration if the operand density is
    sufficiently cromulent.

 Standard options:

    --help

      Print this help.

    --verbose

      Be more verbose. Specify multiple times for more verbosity.

 Options specific to this script:

    [fast] --fast <value>

      If 1, sacrifice accuracy for speed

    [quux.inplace] --quux-inplace <value>

      If 1, do the frobnitz in-place, instead of copying the quux first
      (use with care!)

    [quux.style] --quux-style <value>

      Style of the quux to be frobnitzed (e.g. `cheesy', `purple')
